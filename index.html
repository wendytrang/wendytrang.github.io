   I'M WENDY TRANG A MARKETER A DESIGNER A UCLA BRUIN

THE INS AND OUTS OF THIS SOCAL GAL

I'm a fourth-year student majoring in Economics and minoring in Digital Humanities at UCLA. I am always eager to challenge myself, get things done, and absorb as much knowledge as I can. I enjoy helping others put their ideas onto paper and then meeting the creative challenges to make them a reality. My goal is to work at the intersection of my interests in business, humanities, technology, art, and design.

In my free time, I like to work on personal projects. Though most of what I do is for personal pleasure, I also work as a freelance graphic designer. Art has been my go-to hobby since I was a young girl, so I am excited to get a chance to share it with you all. When I'm not drawing or lettering, I'm on the hunt for delicious ice cream, petting a dog, or (sometimes) traveling the world.
