SPEAK UP BROCHURE

RAISING PARENT VOICES IN LAUSD

Speak UP is a grassroots organization of parents who want a more powerful voice in education policy and strives for an equitable public education. The brochure was created to promote awareness about Speak UP's parent engagement teams across the Los Angeles Union School District. I was tasked with updating the copy and redesigning the brochure while staying consistent with the organizations branding.
