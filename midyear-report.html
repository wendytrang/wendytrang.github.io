SPEAK UP MIDYEAR REPORT

RAISING PARENT VOICES IN LAUSD

The Speak UP United Parents Organization needed to send a midyear report to stakeholders. The report gave a brief overview of the past year and a look ahead at what the new year has in store. Initially, the report was a collection of white pages with diverging images and charts. Working closely with the Director of Operations, I developed a visual template for the report that was consistent with the organization's branding and other materials. I maintained constant communication throughout the design process to ensure we achieved our vision for the report. The final deliverable was published on Issuu for easier sharing with stakeholders as well as parents.

Completed: July 2018
Client: Speak UP
